Defining Need and Audiences

In general, at this point in the process, you should simply identify all the needs without having to come up with solutions. Keep things open and loose.

Target Audience

One thing to point out is that while demographics certainly influence psychographics, they do not predefined them. So, while it's pretty likely that you will find more vegetarian liberals in urban areas on the West Coast of the United States and more gun-toting conservatives in rural Texas, there are definitely exceptions.

In the end of the strategy phase you should summarize all your findings in a strategy document and share it with your clients and your team. You will have figured out the needs of your users and your clients and you will have a deeper understanding about your target audience. The strategy document is also a good place to note other research that you have conducted such as a roundup of similar and inspirational websites and the insight from Analytics data.
